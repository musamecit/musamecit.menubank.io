Last updated: April 08, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MenuBank. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("MenuBank"). Be assured that we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the data necessary to provide our core services, such as:

  • Account Information: If you register, we store your basic profile information.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we use your location to show you nearby restaurants.
  • Usage Data & Analytics: We collect anonymous crash reports and analytics to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Notify you about changes to our Service or your followed restaurants.
  • Provide customer support.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

We offer auto-renewable subscriptions inside the app (e.g. MenuBank Premium Monthly). Payments are processed directly by Apple through your Apple ID account. We do not have access to or store your credit card information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.
