MenuBank

MenuBank is your ultimate guide and social network for discovering, sharing, and tracking restaurant menus. With community-powered pricing updates and real-time reviews, it helps both users explore accurate food prices around them and empowers restaurant owners to officially claim their listings to share official covers, updates, and verified badges.

MenuBank Premium Subscription

Verified restaurant owners can optionally unlock exclusive capabilities such as unlimited menu changes, cover photo updates, and an official verified badge via the auto-renewing "MenuBank Premium Monthly" subscription.
